Our Story

The dawn of AI giants like ChatGPT, Claude, and Bard marked a paradigm shift in the world of artificial intelligence. As industries worldwide raced to incorporate these revolutionary models, the discourse around the pros and cons of AI deepened. While artificial intelligence harbors enormous potential, unchecked deployment could lead to unforeseen consequences. Born from this realization, Plutarch was established to merge AI and education in a safe and responsible manner. Our ethos? Make learning interactive, universally accessible, and delightfully simple.

Why Plutarch?

The seed of our idea was sown when a professor at New York University pointed out a prevailing challenge: the incessant flood of emails in a professor’s inbox. Burdened with an array of questions about lectures, resources, and course content, professors were constantly juggling between teaching and responding. Thus, Plutarch was envisaged to serve as a bridge, enabling students to dive deeper into their lectures and hone their skills, while giving professors some much-needed breathing space.

News

We launched this initiative in Fall 2023. We started with one course, Principles of Data Science at the Undergraduate level taught by Dr. Pascal Wallisch. In Spring 2024, we are expanding this service to three courses at New York University:
1) Principles of Data Science
2) Fundamentals of Machine learning
3) Abnormal Psychology

Our Solutions

Interactive Chat

Elevate your learning experience with our interactive chat feature. Trained on your lecture materials, ChatGPT provides insights akin to conversing with your professor!

Interactive Slides

We take slide explanations a step further. We know students come from all over the world and staying caught up can be difficult. Our interactive slides enable students to ask questions, clarify concepts with a slide-specific explanation.

Practice Quiz

Enhance your understanding with our AI-curated practice quizzes tailored to each lecture. These quizzes serve as invaluable tools for assessment preparation and quick knowledge checks.

Precision Transcript

Our precise transcripts, generated using state-of-the-art speech-to-text models, offer comprehensive and accurate content, catering to students inclined towards textual learning.
